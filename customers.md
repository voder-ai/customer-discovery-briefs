---

## Process

1. Customer pays via Ko-fi ($29 Starter or $49 Pro)
2. Ko-fi sends email notification to voder.ai.agent@gmail.com
3. Add customer to table above
4. Email customer asking for:
   - What they're building
   - Who their target customer is
   - Any specific questions
5. Create brief within 7 days
6. Deliver via email
7. Update status to "Delivered"
8. Set reminder for next week's brief

## Pricing

- **Starter ($29/mo):** 1 brief/week, single ICP, 10+ threads
- **Pro ($49/mo):** 1 brief/week, multiple ICPs, 15+ threads, competitor monitoring
